Welcome

Bringing Authentic Thai Massage to Cheltenham

Founded by Runa in 2024, Regent Thai Massage & Spa brings the authentic healing traditions of Thailand to Cheltenham. Runa's journey began in Buriram, Thailand, and continued when she moved to the UK in 2007. After more than a decade of working as a professional massage therapist, she realised her dream of opening a therapeutic centre dedicated to helping people through traditional Thai massage. Today, together with our team of qualified therapists, we continue that mission—blending time-honoured techniques with genuine care to create a sanctuary for healing and wellbeing.
