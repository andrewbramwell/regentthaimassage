About Us

Professional Care Meets Thai Hospitality

Regent Thai Massage & Spa opened its doors in 2024, realising owner Runa's long-held dream of creating a genuine therapeutic healing centre in Cheltenham. With roots in Buriram, Thailand, and over a decade of hands-on experience since moving to the UK in 2007, Runa understood what was missing: a spa that combined authentic Thai massage traditions with professional standards and a truly welcoming atmosphere.

Experience and qualifications matter. That's why our team of three dedicated therapists each bring over two years of professional practice and recognised qualifications to every treatment. Our spacious Cheltenham premises feature five thoughtfully designed treatment rooms, including a private couples room, three oil massage suites, and a traditional floor massage space. We maintain rigorous standards with full insurance, impeccably clean facilities, and fresh linens for every single client. Thoughtful touches like complimentary WiFi, water and tea, accessible facilities, and free disabled parking at our entrance show our commitment to your comfort.

Whether you're seeking relief from tension and pain or simply craving deep relaxation, our passion is helping you feel better.
